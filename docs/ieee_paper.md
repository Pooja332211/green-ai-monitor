# IEEE Paper: Green AI Monitor - A Comprehensive System for Tracking Carbon Emissions in AI Workloads

## Abstract

*This is a placeholder for the IEEE paper. In a real scenario, this would be a full research paper formatted according to IEEE standards.*

The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies has led to increased concerns about their environmental impact. This paper presents Green AI Monitor, a comprehensive system designed to track and monitor carbon emissions generated by AI workloads in real-time. The system integrates advanced carbon tracking libraries, system resource monitoring, and educational tools to promote sustainable AI practices.
